Who We Serve

The UNESWA Fitness Gym is dedicated to promoting health and wellness for a diverse group of users within and outside the University of Eswatini. Our members include university staff, students, pensioners, and external clients such as SNPF members and individuals under Stop Order arrangements.

Current Challenges

Currently, the gym relies on a manual filing system to manage member information and categorize payments. Physical folders are used to sort data by user group. While this approach has served us, it presents several limitations:

  • Time-consuming processes
  • High risk of human error
  • Difficulties in locating specific information quickly
  • Administrative burden of keeping records updated across categories
  • Lack of scalability as membership grows

Our Digital Solution

To improve efficiency and enhance our services, we are developing a Digital Gym Management System. This modern platform will streamline daily operations by:

  • Handling member registration and categorization
  • Tracking and managing payments
  • Managing bookings for sessions and facilities
  • Generating reports and insights
  • Storing information securely in a centralized system

Benefits of the New System

The shift from a manual to a digital system will result in:

  • Greater accuracy and reduced errors
  • Faster access to member information
  • Improved record-keeping and data security
  • More flexibility to accommodate future growth
  • Enhanced overall experience for gym users and administrators
