Membership Pricing

Category Monthly Fee (SZL) Access
UNESWA Students 150.00 Gym & Pool
UNESWA Staff 300.00 Gym & Pool
Senior Citizens / Pensioners 150.00 Gym Only
External Clients (SNPF / Stop Order) 300.00 Gym & Pool
Company Contract Employees 300.00 Gym Only

Payment Methods

  • Cash Payment (at gym reception)
  • Stop Order (deducted from employer-linked salaries)
  • SNPF Payment Arrangement (approved by management)
Note:
• All new members are required to provide valid identification and proof of eligibility.
• Membership fees are paid monthly in advance.
• Missed payments may result in suspended access until payment is made.
• Membership renewals should be confirmed at the front desk before the start of each new month.
