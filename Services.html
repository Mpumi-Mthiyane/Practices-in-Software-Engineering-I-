1. Clients and User Groups

The UNESWA Fitness Gym serves a range of users including students, staff members, pensioners, SNPF members, and clients under Stop Order arrangements.

2. Information Provided by the User

During Registration:

  • Personal Details: Full name, surname, date of birth, gender, PIN, addresses, contact numbers, occupation, employer (if applicable).
  • Membership Category: Proof such as student ID, staff card, national ID; Institution name (e.g., UNESWA K/L/M Campus).
  • Activities of Interest: Swimming, Aerobics, Weights, Other.
  • Emergency/Health Info: Declaration of physical fitness and absence of medical conditions.

During Payment:

  • Payment Method: Cash, Stop Order, SNPF, etc.
  • Verification: Receipts or bank details as proof.

During Facility Use:

  • Compliance: Agreement to rules like no smoking, pre-swim showers, towel usage.

3. Services Provided by the User

  • Waiver Signing: Electronic signature for liability acceptance.
  • Rule Acknowledgment: Confirmation of understanding gym/pool regulations.
  • Booking Reservations: Classes, swimming sessions, or equipment.
  • Incident Reporting: Notify staff of any violations or safety issues.

4. Resources Provided by the User

  • Hygiene: Personal towel (required per visit), swimming cap.
  • Security: Own padlock for lockers.
  • Attire: Activity-appropriate clothing (e.g., swimwear, gym attire).

5. Facilities Provided by the Gym

  • Exercise Areas: Gymnasium, aerobics equipment, swimming pool, showers, and changing rooms.
  • Support Services: Instructor-led sessions, secure locker access.
  • Admin Support: Membership codes, renewals, and rule enforcement.

6. Timeline of Service Provision

Registration Phase: Provide personal info, eligibility proof, payment, and activity preferences.
Pre-Visit: Book sessions or equipment in advance.
During Visit: Follow gym rules; bring towel, padlock, and proper attire.
Post-Visit: Empty lockers, renew membership, update preferences.

7. Key Compliance Requirements

  • Facility Rules: No nudity, no alcohol or drugs, mandatory swim caps, proper conduct.
  • Validation: Staff may verify IDs, membership, and rule compliance at any time.
